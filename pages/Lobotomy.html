Face the fear, Build the future

Lobotomy Corporation, a management simulation game developed by us. In this game, you take on the role of a manager of a facility that haves creatures known as Abnormalities. The goal is to extract enkephalin from these Abnormalities while managing the risks associated with their containment.

We feature a unique blend of strategy, resource management, and horror elements. Managers must carefully balance the containment of Abnormalities and the extraction of energy to ensure the success for the day.

In Lobotomy Corporation, Abnormalities are the central focus of our game. These dangerous creatures are contained inside the facility, and each one has its own behaviors and requirements for containment.

Players must unlock information and understand the Abnormalities in order to effectively manage them. Some Abnormalities may require specific gear or work types to handle safely.
